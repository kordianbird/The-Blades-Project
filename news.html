What's New With Our Band

Our lead singer Steven just adopted a retired greyhound!

Steven loves animals so it's no surprise that he finally adopted a retired greyhound which he always wanted. Right now he owns two Huskys, one German Shephard, a bunny and now also a Greyhound!

The Blades are planning a tour in Europe!

The Blades have now toured around Ireland twice but with the band's growing popularity it's about time to take a tour around Europe. As of right now they are planning to visit their fans in Germany, Sweden, Poland and France although some countries will be added to the list considering the band's growing fanbase.

Ozzy Osbourne is inspired by The Blades!

In a recent interview with Ozzy Osbourne he was asked what inspired him to make his new album "Ordinary Man". Ozzy responded "I took a break from singing in 2019 and spent some time browsing trending songs on Spotify to listen to, I came across "The Blades" and I immediately got hooked on them. Their song writing really immpressed me and inspired me to make another album"

Is Steven going to make his own single?

Don't worry! Steven isn't leaving The Blades but he has always wanted to release his own song to show his fans that he really enjoys writing songs and that he can write something just as good as the band's already impressive songs.
