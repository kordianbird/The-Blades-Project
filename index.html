The Blades

The Blades have sold over 2 million copies of their first album "sharper than blades" in Ireland and have currently sold 4 million copies of their new album "happy souls" world wide. Their song "Blast" was the 3rd best song on officialcharts.com in 2019 which helped them gain popularity in other countries. The Blades have went on 2 all Ireland tours in 2019 and 2020 with more tours to come in the future and possibly an all Europe tour sometime in summer of 2021.
